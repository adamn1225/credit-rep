Bulk Upload Accounts

Import multiple derogatory accounts from a CSV file

How to use
  1. Download the CSV template below
  2. Fill in your account information (one account per row)
  3. Save the file and upload it here
Step 1: Download Template

Get the CSV template with sample data and proper formatting

Download CSV Template
Required Columns:
  • bureau - Credit bureau name (Experian, TransUnion, Equifax)
  • creditor_name - Name of the creditor
  • account_number - Account number (last 4 digits recommended)
  • reason - Dispute reason
Optional Columns:
  • account_type - Type of account (Credit Card, Auto Loan, etc.)
  • balance - Account balance (numeric)
  • notes - Additional notes
Step 2: Upload Your CSV

Select your completed CSV file to import accounts

Tips:
  • You can edit the template in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet application
  • Delete the sample rows and add your own account information
  • Make sure to save as CSV format (not Excel .xlsx)
  • Bureau names must match exactly: Experian, TransUnion, or Equifax
  • All accounts will be added with "pending" status
  • You can upload multiple times - duplicate accounts won't be created
CSV Format Example
bureau creditor_name account_number account_type balance reason notes
Experian Capital One 1234 Credit Card 1500.00 Not my account Never opened this
TransUnion Chase Bank 5678 Auto Loan 8500.50 Already paid Paid in full 2023
Equifax Discover 9012 Credit Card 2300.00 Incorrect balance Should be $1000
