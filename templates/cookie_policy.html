Cookie Policy

Effective Date: December 3, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, phone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, login status, and usage patterns.

Next Credit uses cookies to provide a secure, personalized experience and improve our service.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies (Required)

These cookies are necessary for the platform to function and cannot be disabled:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
session Flask session cookie for user authentication and login state 30 days (persistent)
tour_completed Tracks whether you've completed the product tour Permanent (localStorage)
CSRF Token Security token to prevent cross-site request forgery attacks Session (deleted when you close browser)

2.2 Functional Cookies (Optional)

These cookies enhance your experience but are not strictly necessary:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
theme_preference Remembers your light/dark mode preference (if implemented) 1 year
language Remembers your language preference (if applicable) 1 year

2.3 Analytics Cookies (Optional - Currently Not Used)

We currently do NOT use third-party analytics cookies (Google Analytics, etc.). If we add analytics in the future, we will:

  • Notify you and update this policy
  • Provide an opt-out option
  • Anonymize data where possible

2.4 Marketing Cookies (Not Used)

We do NOT use marketing or advertising cookies. We do not track you across websites or share cookie data with third-party advertisers.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Next Credit uses the following third-party services that may set their own cookies:

3.1 Plaid (Banking Integration - Optional)

If you connect bank accounts via Plaid, Plaid may set cookies for authentication. See Plaid's Privacy Policy.

3.2 OpenAI (AI Processing)

OpenAI processes dispute letters but does NOT set cookies on your device.

3.3 Lob (Certified Mail)

Lob handles mail delivery but does NOT set cookies on your device.

4. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: Keep you logged in across page visits
  • Security: Prevent unauthorized access and CSRF attacks
  • User Experience: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Session Management: Track your active session and prevent session hijacking

5. Managing Cookies

5.1 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

Warning: Blocking essential cookies will prevent you from logging in and using Next Credit.

5.2 Clearing Cookies

You can delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Note that this will log you out and reset your preferences.

6. Session Security

Our session cookies have the following security features:

  • HTTPS Only: Session cookies are only transmitted over encrypted connections (production)
  • HttpOnly Flag: Cookies cannot be accessed by JavaScript (prevents XSS attacks)
  • SameSite Protection: Cookies are restricted to same-site requests (prevents CSRF attacks)
  • 30-Day Expiration: Sessions automatically expire after 30 days of inactivity

7. LocalStorage & SessionStorage

In addition to cookies, we use browser storage for non-sensitive data:

  • localStorage: Stores tour completion status (persists across sessions)
  • sessionStorage: Temporary data for current session only (cleared when you close the tab)

This data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers unless explicitly needed.

8. Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. Next Credit respects DNT signals:

  • We do NOT use third-party tracking or advertising cookies
  • We do NOT share cookie data with advertisers or data brokers
  • Your usage data is only used to improve our service (not sold to third parties)

9. Children's Privacy

Next Credit is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect cookies or data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as we add new features or comply with regulations. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date."
